Did you know that Ten Sigma offers a full-service, online transition support system?
 
The Ten Sigma TRAX Online Transition System is a comprehensive transition system for students with disabilities that are mild, moderate, sever, or on the autism spectrum. The TRAX software includes modules for transition surveys, rubrics, activities, plans with multiple year progress reports, annual goal management and data collection. This user-friendly software provides transition teachers with all they need to meet the transition needs of their students.
