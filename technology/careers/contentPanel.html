Senior Programmer

We are currently seeking a Senior Developer for our data warehouse team to focus on web and database development to continue to create, enhance, support and deliver a leading, successful BI solution for K-12 educators. The Senior Data Warehouse/Business Intelligence Developer will work as a member of cmERDC's data warehouse team. This position leads or participates in the full development life cycle to enhance the BI capabilities (create or modify reports, dashboards, scorecards, self-service, etc.) of the data warehouse environment used by our customers. This position works directly with the Data Warehouse Product Manager to deliver value through BI to our K-12 education customers.

Responsibilities:

  • Creates or modifies reports, dashboards, scorecards, self-service applications, data visualization applications, etc.

  • Works with product manager to analyze BI requirements

  • Develops proofs of concept to validate ideas or technologies

  • Provides best practice standards and templates

  • Troubleshoots BI related problems and tunes for performance

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor's degree

  • 5+ years of experience in Information Technology

  • Experience translating business requirements into reports, dashboard/scorecards, and self-service options

  • Experience working on data warehouse projects

  • Experience working with BI tool architectures, functions, and features

  • Experience working with SQL, SQL stored procedures, and relational/multidimensional databases

  • Experience in one or more of the follow areas: Data analysis and data modeling, Data acquisition (ETL/ELT), Testing

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Experience with the Microsoft Business Intelligence Stack: SQL Server 2008 R2 and 2012 Enterprise Edition, SSAS, SSIS

  • ASP.NET framework (C#, JavaScript, and HTML)

  • Visual Studio, SubVersion and MDX

  • Strong problem-solving and a strong customer service orientation

  • Ability to manage multiple competing tasks at the same time

Support Specialist

The Learning Solutions Team provides PK-12 software solutions and services to school districts and cooperatives. This position is responsible for delivering support, implementation and data integration services to customers of cmERDC. Services can include consulting, training, data analysis, data loading, special reporting, implementation management and other services.

Responsibilities:

  • Application Expertise − Provide application, content expertise and support to customers of cmERDC's Student Plans and other software solutions. Remain current in the industry and in technology.

  • Customer Advocate and Liaison − Must act as the key resource available to customers to help them resolve application issues that they have encountered and collect feedback on application needs and customer service concerns. Interface with customer leadership including superintendents, principals, assistant principals, department managers, special education directors, curriculum directors, and technology directors. Provide regular written and verbal communications to management, team and customers concerning projects and efforts. May participate in user meetings, state meetings, and other meetings.

  • Debugging & Support − Provide support for medium to complex software problems resulting from incorrect setup, program defects, data issues or design problems that are not handled by the software related to Student Plans and other applications. Identify and document enhancement suggestions and user requirements as they are generated by users. Participate in testing for pre-release versions of software.

  • Data Manipulation and Conversion − May be required to prepare data from a Student Information Systems (SIS) and other customer data sources for import into cmERDC software applications using a variety of tools including our ETL solution, spreadsheets, MS SQL utilities and more. May be required to write utilities and reports using database and reporting software and/or tools, in order to facilitate the functioning of applications and to generate special reports.

  • Service Coordination − Provide implementation management services for the installation of cmERDC applications. May act as the primary customer contact during implementations. Must manage customer expectations and ensure customer satisfaction. Coordinate activities with customers and business partners to ensure that customer's software solutions are installed successfully and on time. Maintain customer database and work to enhance a knowledge database of support experience. May provide reports and statistics to customers and management. May coordinate acquisition, implementation and support of technology with other vendors.

  • Training and Documentation − Develop, enhance and deliver training to customers on software applications. Create and maintain documentation to support training, usage and version releases of applications and utilities.

  • Leadership − May provide work direction for other Team members on particular projects. May receive direction from Application Product Managers.

  • Other − Perform other duties as assigned.

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor's Degree in MIS or related field preferred. Two−year degree in MIS or related field minimum. Up to eight years of additional technology support related experience may be used in lieu of higher education (two years of experience in lieu of each year of higher education).

  • Minimum of three years of school district experience working in the area of Special Education. Minimum of one year of experience providing support for Special Education applications.

  • Ability to work with limited supervision. Individual should be self-directed. May participate in planning for the delivery of one or more support services in the assigned Product Area.

  • Good written communications skills required. May produce reports and correspondence for staff and customers.

  • Good verbal communications skills. Required to listen carefully and display considerable tact in answering questions and relaying information and general communications to customers. Ability to communicate technical concepts to non−technical customers. Ability to communicate effectively with staff and customers at all levels including those at top managerial levels. Must give directions to staff as well as communicate with school district staff and management regarding service activities.

  • Ability to solve software/user problems of a medium to high level of complexity, requiring analysis to find solutions to difficult technical problems. Ability to provide creative solutions for problems without obvious answers. Ability to concentrate on multiple problem situations and pressures of deadlines. Ability to address and solve problems unique to a school district.

  • Must maintain an awareness of current and future trends in education and computer applications technology. Knowledge of hardware and software systems in use by the customer base. Ability to work with medium to high level of technical information.

  • Must maintain a high level of knowledge of cmERDC applications. Must be skilled in word processing, spreadsheet, database and server operating systems.

  • Display a high level of professionalism. Exhibit courtesy and tact toward staff and customers. Must maintain absolute confidentiality with customer data and information.

  • Ability to handle details with a high degree of accuracy and to support multiple users at once. Ability to organize, set priorities, handles multiple tasks, identifies importance and maintain flexibility.

  • Driver's license required. Time may be spent outside the office assisting customers at their location.
