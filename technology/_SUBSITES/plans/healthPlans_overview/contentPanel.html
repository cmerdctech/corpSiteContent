The Student Plans System provides:
Create Individual Health Plans!
School nurses can develop IHPs that meet the needs of students and clearly document the nursing diagnosis, student goals and outcomes related to their health condition, nursing interventions to achieve those goals.
Sunrise River Press Nursing Library!
This extensive library produced and maintained by Sunrise River Press has been converted to an electronic format to be easily accessed by nurses when creating health plans.
Create Emergency Care Plans!
Emergency Care Plans can be created as a standalone document or in conjunction with the IHP. These plans include the assessment and symptoms of the condition and what should do or not do if the symptoms occur in a classroom, bus, lunchroom, etc
