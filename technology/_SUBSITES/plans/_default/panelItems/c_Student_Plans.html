Student Plans
Student Plans is a web-based application that allows users to complete all Due Process requirements for Special Education students.
Flexible configuration offers additional modules for Health Plans, Transportation, MA Billing and Academic Improvement Plans (RTI, CLP, ILP, ELL) as well as an interface to Viewpoint (data warehouse), Ten Sigma's TRAX (online transition solution).
Student Plans is carefully designed and maintained to mee all Minnesota requirements. A system-level Completeness Check insures that all requirements are met.
User definable document sections and customizable document templates make Student Plans the choice to match every school district's unique requirements.
