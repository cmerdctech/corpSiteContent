Data Collection for Educational Assessments
Expressbook offers a wide range of features to insure efficient, accurate insights into your students' performance.
Organizes and Promulgates Standards
Create complex outlines of standards and markable activities. Each can be assigned to one or more grade levels, associated with one or more specialties, have it's own mark scale.
Facilitates Collaboration
A homeroom teacher can create a list of students and assign it some markable activities and then delegate the new class to another teacher. Great for everything from reading groups to field trips.
Creates Specialty Classes
it's easy to assign specialty classes with Expressbook. A quick, easy tool let's you assign a specialty teacher to one class, a grade level or the entire school.
Generates Report Cards
Different format and content for each grade. And, it runs on a separate server so you don't have the end of term computer slowdown conflict.
Oh! And It is a Grade Book, too!
Mark teacher-created activities. Speed marking by selecting students and applying a mark to a group. Sort, select, filter, delegate. Designed by teachers for teachers.
