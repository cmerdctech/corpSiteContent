How to join


Membership can be secured in one of three ways:
One-Year Membership Item No. Price
1. Individual membership W-420 $65
2. Five-user license W-421 $300
3. District license N/A Negotiated - please call.

To join call 800.657.3815, email info@tensigma.org, or fax your order to 507.345.7574. We accept purchase orders, Visa, or Mastercard.

What's inside?


Access to complete units of instruction for the 65 transition skills in the series for higher-functioning students and the 61 transition skills in the series for students with moderate disabilities. These are the same activities as are in the Activities for Transition I and Activities for Transition II series.

Benefits of membership


With membership, you get unlimited online access to more than 900 transition tools for students who are higher-functioning (anticipate living independent adult lives). You also get more than 800 transition tools for students with moderate disabilities. Membership to the activities is for one year, with a reduced membership price for the second year and beyond.
