TenSigma Transition System


The Ten Sigma transition system is designed to help transition teachers do a better job of meeting the transition needs of their students. Whether you implement the system using the hard copy material or TRAX Online Transition Software the system is the same.

Research-Based Transition Skills

Start with four comprehensive sets of transition skills that are specifically designed to meet the transition needs of students with four types disabilities

(1) mild,
(2) moderate,
(3) severe, or
(4) on the autism spectrum.
