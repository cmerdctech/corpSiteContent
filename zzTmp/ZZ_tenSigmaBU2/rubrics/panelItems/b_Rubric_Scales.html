Rubric Scales Determine How a Rubric Will Be Used


In most cases, the wording for a rubric stays the same. It is the scale that changes that determines how a rubric will be used.

One of the most important types of rubrics is the Assessment Rubric which is used to determine whether a student possesses an important skill.

A second type of rubric is the Trials rubric which is used to monitoring progress.

A third type of rubric is the Progress rubric which is used to display progress in developing skills.

These three types of rubrics work in combination to enhance the process of writing annual measureable annual goals and monitoring and reporting progress.
