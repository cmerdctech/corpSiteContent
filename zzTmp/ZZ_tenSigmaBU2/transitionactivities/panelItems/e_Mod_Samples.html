Moderate Disabilities Transition Activities


The Activities for Transition II: for Students with Moderate Disabilities series is designed to teach transition skills to individuals who anticipate living and working somewhat dependently as adults.
For each skill in the moderate disabilities series there is a complete unit of instruction. This unit includes a variety of tools and activities designed for the student with moderate disabilities, including:

Contents of Activity Units


Each unit includes a variety of tools and activities, including:

  • Cover page
  • Unit table of contents that can be used to track progress in completing a unit.
  • Rubric that defines the particular skill.
  • Overview of the skill.
  • Variety of activities (8 to 12 activities in each unit).
    1. Overview activity
    2. Research
    3. Definitions
    4. Interviews
    5. Observations
    6. Practice activities
    7. Evaluations/plans for improvement
    8. Scenarios (simulated real-life)
    9. Authentic activities (real-life)
  • Certificates that can be earned.
    1. Certificate of Strength
    2. Certificate of Accomplishment
