Activities for Transition I: for Higher-Functioning Students


Learn More about High Fuctioning Skills     
The Activities for Transition I: for Higher-Functioning Students series is designed to teach transition skills to individuals who anticipate living and working independently as adults.
For each skill in the higher-functioning series there is a complete unit of instruction that is designed to teach the research-based criteria of the skill as defined by the corresponding rubric included in the Rubrics for Transition I: for Higher-Functioning Students manual.
The 65 units of instruction included in the higher-functioning series are compiled into a seven-volume set of manuals.
