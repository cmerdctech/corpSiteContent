Higher-Functioning Transition Activities


The Activities for Transition I: for Higher-Functioning Students series is designed to teach transition skills to individuals who anticipate living and working independently as adults.
For each skill in the higher-functioning series there is a complete unit of instruction. This unit includes a variety of tools and activities, including:

Contents of Activity Units


Each unit includes a variety of tools and activities, including:

  • Cover page
  • Unit table of contents that can be used to track progress in completing a unit.
  • Rubric that defines the particular skill.
  • Overview of the skill.
  • Variety of activities (8 to 12 activities in each unit).
    1. Overview activity
    2. Research
    3. Definitions
    4. Interviews
    5. Observations
    6. Practice activities
    7. Evaluations/plans for improvement
    8. Scenarios (simulated real-life)
    9. Authentic activities (real-life)
  • Certificates that can be earned.
    1. Certificate of Strength
    2. Certificate of Accomplishment
