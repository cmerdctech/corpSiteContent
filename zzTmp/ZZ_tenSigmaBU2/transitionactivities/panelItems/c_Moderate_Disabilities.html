Activities for Transition II: for Students with Moderate Disabilities


Learn More about Moderate Disabilities Skills     
The Activities for Transition II: for Students with Moderate Disabilities series is designed to teach transition skills to individuals who anticipate living and working somewhat dependently as adults.
For each skill in the moderate disabilities series there is a complete unit of instruction for that is designed to teach the research-based criteria of the skill as defined by the corresponding rubric included in the Rubrics for Transition II: for Students with Moderate Disabilities manual.
The 61 units of instruction included in the moderate disabilities series are compiled into a seven-volume set of manuals.
