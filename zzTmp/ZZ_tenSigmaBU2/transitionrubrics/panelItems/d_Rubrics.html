Rubrics


The manual contains two rubrics for each of the 232 skills.

Click to See Larger Image

The first type of rubric is the Assessment rubric which is used to assess how well the student is able to meet expectations or demonstrate mastery on a skill. This type of rubric is often used to define the student's present level for selected skills.

The second type of rubric is the Progress rubric. It is used to document and report progress on a quarterly basis.
