Rubrics are Valuable Educational Tools


Rubrics are guides that display the specific details for what a student should demonstrate in meeting expectations, showing mastery, or in achieving proficiency. Rubrics assist teachers in:
  • Communicating expectations
  • Writing more measurable IEPs
  • Focusing instruction
  • Monitoring progress and reporting progress
