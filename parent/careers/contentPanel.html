Office Equipment / Copier Technician

The office Equipment/Copier Technician position is a highly skilled technician level where the employee is capable of a full workload. The individual is responsible for the timely and cost effective maintenance and repair of office product equipment and for promoting and maintaining a high level of customer satisfaction with our service and Kyocera branded office product equipment that is offered by cmERDC.

Work type: Weekdays, 40 hours/week 7:30 AM to 3:30 PM

Benefits: Health, Dental, Life, Long Term Disability, Paid Holidays, Vacation, Sick, PERA (Retirement)

Skills and Experience

  • Maximize equipment up-time through timely response and effective and efficient repair with selective customer training to ensure high levels of customer satisfaction.

  • Communicate and interact with customers, field service, sales, management, and administration in a way that is courteous, positive and professional.

  • Complete all administrative aspects of the job on time and error-free.

  • Provide accurate feedback with call close information, parts usage reporting and customer meter readings.

  • Mechanical and electrical aptitude and demonstrated skills.

  • Prior experience in maintenance and repair of copier office equipment preferred.

  • Excellent customer service and communication skills.

  • Ability to troubleshoot and think independently.

  • Knowledge of Basic Networking preferred.

  • Computer and analytical skills.

  • High School Diploma or G.E.D. equivalent a minimum.

  • Ability to life and carry 50 pounds.

  • Pull, lift, reach and transport equipment parts and boxes.

  • Must be able to problem solve, conduct written and verbal communication, read and comprehend technical information.

  • Must have valid Minnesota driver's license.

  • Perform other duties as assigned that support cmERDC service organization.

Support Specialist

The Learning Solutions Team provides PK-12 software solutions and services to school districts and cooperatives. This position is responsible for delivering data loading and support services to K-12 education data warehouse customers of cmERDC. Services can include data conversion, manipulation and loading, troubleshooting, testing, software versioning, implementation management, customer support, training and other services.

Responsibilities:

  • Data Manipulation, Conversion and Loading − Required to convert data from a Student Information Systems (SIS) and other customer data sources into the data warehouse solution using a variety of tools including our ETL solution, spreadsheets, MS SQL utilities, data extractor utilities and more. Required to install and configure utilities, write queries and reports using programming software and/or tools in order to facilitate the functioning of applications and to generate special reports.

  • Support − Required to provide phone, email and onsite application support to customers. Must be able to analyze and resolve data loading problems resulting from incorrect setup, program defects, data defects and requirements not handled by applications. Must succeed in building positive relationships with school districts and others.

  • Testing − Test programs, enhancements, software application versions to insure they meet our requirement specifications.

  • Database − Must be experienced with SQL database technology, especially the Microsoft SQL database (2008 R2). Must be able to work with data using a variety of software utilities. May be required to work with utilities and programs to facilitate the functioning of applications and to generate special reports.

  • Documentation − May need to provide technical and user documentation in support of the data loading and data warehouse applications developed for customers.

  • Change Management − May need to organize software code versions and bundle releases using SourceSafe, Microsoft Visual Studio, Subversion and other technology utilities.

  • Implementation Management − Must manage customer expectations and ensure customer satisfaction. Coordinate activities with customers to ensure installation is successful and on time. May provide implementation management services for the installation and data loading of our data warehousing solution. Act as a customer contact during implementations.

  • Training − May be required to provide customer and other support staff training, which may require travel.

  • Analysis and Design − May need to analyze the application needs of K-12 customers and design software solutions appropriate to the user needs. Provide content knowledge and expertise to customers. Remain current in the industry and in technology.

Provide regular written and verbal communications to management, team and customers concerning projects and efforts. Provide input into team planning and direction.
