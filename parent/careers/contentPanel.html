SMART Systems Customer Support III

cmERDC is seeking a dynamic, self-starter to assist with support of our HR applications within SMART Systems.

Responsibilities:

  • SMART Systems Support with a focus on HR applications.

  • Participate in the SMART System Software Development Team, which will include software development and enhancements, analysis and testing.

  • Semiannual user training and new customer training on SMART Systems and Public School payroll reporting requirements.

  • Coordinates with 3rd party software applications that integrate into SMART Systems and trains member districts on the setup and use of such applications.

  • Assist with the creation of documentation on the use of SMART Systems.

  • Establish communication protocols for customer outreach.

 Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor's Degree and some experience in a job-related field OR equivalent combination of education, training, and experience in a job-related field that enables performance of all aspects of this position.

  • Comprehensive knowledge of the principles and practices of HR/Payroll.

  • Strong understanding of school district reporting requirements.

  • High level of writing, speaking and listening skills.

  • Knowledge of MS Excel, MS Word and PowerPoint.

  • The ability to prepare and deliver presentations or training sessions to large audiences.

  • Strong critical thinking skills.

Data Integration Specialist

We are currently seeking a Data Integration Specialist to design, implement and support integration solutions for our K-12 software applications. The Data Integration Specialist position will work with Product Managers and software developers to deliver value to our K-12 education customers.

Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, implement and maintain ETL (extract, transform, load) processes to extract data from a Student Information System (SIS) and other customer data sources and convert extracted data into formats required by various cmERDC solutions.

  • Support and monitor ETL processes which can be installed at the customer or internally at cmERDC.

  • Ensure data accuracy.

  • Conduct root cause analysis and resolve production problems and data issues.

  • Design, implement, and maintain web (and desktop) based solutions to enhance and monitor the ETL processes.

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor's Degree in MIS or related field required. Additional technology related experience may be used in lieu of higher education (two years of experience in lieu of each year of higher education).

  • Experience working with data stored in a relational database environment. Minimum of two years' experience or equivalent proficiency with SQL database technology.

  • Experience installing and configuring applications in a Microsoft Windows environment.

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Experience with school district data.

  • Experience with Microsoft SQL Server 2008 R2 and 2012 Enterprise Edition including Microsoft SSIS.

  • Programming experience using C# and Microsoft Visual Studio (2010 or later).

  • Strong problem-solving skills.

  • Ability to manage multiple competing tasks at the same time.
