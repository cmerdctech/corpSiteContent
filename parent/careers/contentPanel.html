Senior Programmer

We are currently seeking a Senior Developer for our data warehouse team to focus on web and database development to continue to create, enhance, support and deliver a leading, successful BI solution for K-12 educators. The Senior Data Warehouse/Business Intelligence Developer will work as a member of cmERDC's data warehouse team. This position leads or participates in the full development life cycle to enhance the BI capabilities (create or modify reports, dashboards, scorecards, self-service, etc.) of the data warehouse environment used by our customers. This position works directly with the Data Warehouse Product Manager to deliver value through BI to our K-12 education customers.

Responsibilities:

  • Creates or modifies reports, dashboards, scorecards, self-service applications, data visualization applications, etc.

  • Works with product manager to analyze BI requirements

  • Develops proofs of concept to validate ideas or technologies

  • Provides best practice standards and templates

  • Troubleshoots BI related problems and tunes for performance

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor's degree

  • 5+ years of experience in Information Technology

  • Experience translating business requirements into reports, dashboard/scorecards, and self-service options

  • Experience working on data warehouse projects

  • Experience working with BI tool architectures, functions, and features

  • Experience working with SQL, SQL stored procedures, and relational/multidimensional databases

  • Experience in one or more of the follow areas: Data analysis and data modeling, Data acquisition (ETL/ELT), Testing

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Experience with the Microsoft Business Intelligence Stack: SQL Server 2008 R2 and 2012 Enterprise Edition, SSAS, SSIS

  • ASP.NET framework (C#, JavaScript, and HTML)

  • Visual Studio, SubVersion and MDX

  • Strong problem-solving and a strong customer service orientation

  • Ability to manage multiple competing tasks at the same time
