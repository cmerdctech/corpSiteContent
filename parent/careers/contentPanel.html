Business Systems Analyst


SMART Systems Development provides financial and human resource software that is currently deployed in more than 300 school districts throughout Minnesota. This position will serve as the payroll and mandated tax expert for development changes to the SMART software suite. The position will act as a liaison among project sponsors/end-users and developers to elicit, analyze, communicate, and validate requirements for changes and enhancements to the software.


Responsibilities:

  • Determines operational objectives by studying business functions, gathering information and evaluating output requirements

  • Defines project/task requirements by identifying project/task milestones, phases, and elements

  • Monitors and documents progress by tracking activity, resolving problems and communicating with developers, users and sponsors

  • Provides references for users by writing and maintaining user documentation, providing help desk support and training users

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor degree in information technology or business related degree

  • Experience with mandated tax changes

  • Working knowledge of relational databases, specifically SQL Server 2008

  • Experience with software development methodologies

  • Effective written and oral communication skills sufficient to lead discussions and deliver technical information to both technical and non-technical personnel

  • Experience with school district payroll processing
