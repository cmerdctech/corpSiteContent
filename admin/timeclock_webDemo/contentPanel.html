TimeClock Plus Live Web Demo Schedule - Featuring new ACA functionality

You are invited to participate in a Web demonstration of TimeClock Plus Software.

This demo will include a brief overview of the TimeClock Plus Web Edition hosted and supported by the MN Regional Centers. The TimeClock Plus Web Edition is a full featured browser based time and labor management application that provides robust and powerful employee time and attendance tracking on the web.

Also included in this demo will be the new Benefit Status Monitor used for ACA (Affordable Care Act) compliance. The Benefit Status Monitor for Timeclock Plus will alleviate the time, money and risk involved in determining the status of variable hour employees by providing you with automated tools that can be used to verify an employee's status.
